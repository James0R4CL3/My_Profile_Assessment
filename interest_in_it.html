Interest In I.T




My interest in I.T and when it started

My interest in I.T started when I was about 7 years old; I was given an awful laptop by today's standards from a family friend that didn't want it anymore.
It was old and slow but it could play music and 1 car game,that was enough to pique my interest.
I spent weeks going through all the setting and just learning everything I could about how it worked.

My current I.T interest is programming, gaming, sound engineering and graphic design.
Programming is a new-found passion for me, when I started this course I knew I wanted to work in the industry but I wasn't sure what I wanted to do exactly.
Since starting this course and learning to code I have found myself really enjoying it and would love to work in software development someday.


Why I chose to study at RMIT

I chose RMIT because the I.T degree covers a lot of different parts of I.T and I wanted to find exactly what I would enjoy doing the most in I.T,
being able to study such a broad amount of subjects seems like the best way for me to learn more skills and find my passion in the field.


What I expect to learn during my studies

During my degree, I expect to learn a broad skill set and also learn what I enjoy doing in I.T the most.
I will learn how to study from home and learn more discipline to ensure I stay on track with my progress.
I am very excited to learn more about programming, web development and networking!
